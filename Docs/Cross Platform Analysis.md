# Meta Ad Performance Dashboard | Cross-Platform Comparison Analysis

## Executive Comparison Summary
Both Facebook and Instagram campaigns demonstrate strong top-of-funnel performance, generating high engagement, click activity, and audience reach. However, both platforms experience lower-funnel conversion inefficiencies, with substantial drop-off occurring between click and purchase stages.

### Overall Platform Observation
-	Facebook generates higher reach, clicks, and purchases 
-	Instagram generates strong engagement intensity and audience interaction 
-	Both platforms maintain similar CTR and engagement performance 
-	Conversion efficiency remains limited across both platforms 

## KPI Performance Comparison

| Metric | Facebook | Instagram |
|---|---|---|
| Impressions | 216K | 123.8K |
| Clicks | 25.4K | 14.7K |
| Purchases | 1.3K | 708 |
| CTR | 11.76% | 11.86% |
| Engagement Rate | 13.56% | 13.60% |
| Conversion Rate | 5.21% | 4.82% |
| Purchase Rate | 0.61% | 0.57% |

### Insight
Facebook delivers stronger overall campaign scale and purchase output, while Instagram demonstrates slightly stronger engagement and click-through performance.

## Funnel Performance Comparison

### Shared Observation
Both platforms experience the largest performance decline between click and purchase stages.

### Insight
Top-of-funnel acquisition performance is strong across Meta campaigns, but lower-funnel conversion efficiency remains the primary performance limitation.

### Business Implication
Campaign creatives and targeting strategies are successfully generating interest and traffic, but post-click conversion performance requires optimization across both platforms.

## Audience Comparison

### Shared Audience Trends
Both platforms show:
-	Highest engagement among users aged 18–30 
-	Stronger engagement from female audiences 
-	Declining interaction among users above age 35 

### Insight
Campaign messaging and creative direction are consistently more effective among younger female audience segments across both platforms.

### Business Implication
The campaign strategy is highly concentrated around a narrow demographic group, limiting broader audience penetration.

## Ad Format Comparison

### Facebook
-	Video and Stories formats deliver strongest overall performance 
-	Static Image formats demonstrate weaker conversion efficiency 

### Instagram
-	Video formats demonstrate strongest purchase efficiency 
-	Stories support stronger conversion performance 
-	Image formats generate strong click activity 
-	Carousel formats maintain stable engagement performance 

#### Insight
Dynamic ad formats consistently outperform static formats across both platforms.

#### Business Implication
Video and Stories creatives are the strongest-performing content formats within the Meta campaign ecosystem.

## Overall Business Insights

- **Strong Awareness and Engagement Performance**  
Both platforms demonstrate strong top-of-funnel effectiveness, generating high impressions, traffic volume, and audience interaction.

- **Lower-Funnel Conversion Limitation**  
Despite strong engagement performance, conversion efficiency remains comparatively weaker, indicating friction within the purchase journey.

- **Audience Concentration Dependency**  
Campaign performance is primarily driven by younger female audience segments across both platforms, highlighting a reliance on a narrow demographic group.

- **Dynamic Creative Advantage**  
Video and Stories formats consistently deliver stronger engagement and conversion outcomes compared to more static ad formats.

## Cross-Platform Recommendations

1. **Strengthen Conversion Funnel Performance**
- Optimize landing page experience to ensure a seamless and frictionless purchase journey.
- Improve CTA clarity and consistency across all campaigns to drive stronger conversion intent.

2. **Enhance Retargeting Strategy**
- Re-engage users who have shown intent through clicks or engagement but did not complete purchases.
- Implement structured remarketing campaigns focused on conversion recovery and personalized offers.

3. **Scale High-Performing Ad Formats**
- Increase investment in Video and Stories formats across both platforms due to their consistent performance advantage.
- Gradually reduce reliance on lower-performing static creatives to improve overall campaign efficiency.

4. **Improve Audience Segmentation**
- Prioritize high-performing younger demographic groups across both platforms.
- Develop tailored messaging and creatives for underperforming audience segments to improve relevance and reach.

5. **Optimize Campaign Timing**
- Concentrate budget allocation during peak engagement periods to maximize reach and conversion potential.
- Reduce ad spend during lower-response periods across both platforms to improve efficiency.
